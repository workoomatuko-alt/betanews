How It Works

End-to-end, fully automated

Four stages run automatically, six times a day, with no human in the loop.

📡

Fetch

Pulls 45 articles from Ynet, Walla & Walla Sports RSS feeds every 2 hours

🤖

Process

Claude AI selects the top 12 stories, ranks by priority tier, translates to Amharic

🎙️

Voice

Google TTS generates a 2-minute Amharic audio brief, accessible to all literacy levels

✈️

Deliver

Text update + voice note sent to the Telegram group from 9am to 7pm Israel time
